What We Offer

For all parents, the education of their children is of the greatest importance and selecting the right school is not an easy task. the whole purpose of education is to turn mirrors into windows

The School was established in 1979 over these values and ethos of the founder:

  1. An appropriate academic challenge which enables students to realize his/her poteltial.
  2. An environment which promotes good work ethos and encourages all students to acquire a critical approach to learning.
  3. Infrastructural facilities which not only make learning enjoyable, but also stress-free and effective
  4. All-round development of the children so that not only do they establish themselves in their lives, but also achieve a higher moral status and become good citizens.
